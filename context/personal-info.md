### Why This Product

Built the voice dictation app to solve his own problem. Running multiple businesses means writing thousands of words daily—typing was the bottleneck.

Tested every dictation tool on the market (Wispr Flow, Apple Dictation, Google Voice Typing). None met the standard for speed, accuracy, and workflow integration. Built his own, first for personal use, then for the WEDOHYPE and Liberators teams.

The app proved faster than anything else available. Added audio file upload for transcribing voice memos and meetings—a workflow gap no competitor addressed. Positive internal feedback led to the decision to launch commercially.
