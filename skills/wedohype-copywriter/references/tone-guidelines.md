# Tone Guidelines

Industry-specific voice profiles, vocabulary patterns, and register guidelines. Read when adapting copy for a specific industry.

---

## Medical / Health

**Voice:** Empathetic + Reassuring + Expert
**Register:** Formal (εσείς)
**Emotional/Rational mix:** 70% emotional (fear reduction), 30% rational (clinical proof)

### Tone Markers
- Reassurance words: ασφαλής, ανώδυνη, χωρίς άγχος, σιγουριά, εμπιστοσύνη
- Expert credibility: credentials, certifications, years of experience, publications
- Patient-first framing: "Η υγεία σας βρίσκεται στα χέρια εξειδικευμένων γιατρών"

### Vocabulary Examples
- "Εξατομικευμένη θεραπεία" not "λύσεις υγείας"
- "Κλινικά αποδεδειγμένο" not "αποτελεσματικό"
- Keep English medical terms: "Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD)", "CEREC", "CT scan"

### Pain Point Approach
- Symptom recognition lists (specific, not vague)
- "Μην αγνοείς τα σημάδια..."
- Address fear directly but gently

### CTA Style
- "Κλείσε ραντεβού" / "Επικοινωνήστε μαζί μας"
- Formal (εσείς) in CTAs
- Low-pressure: "Ας μιλήσουμε" for sensitive topics (post-surgery, dental fear)

### What to Avoid
- Overpromising ("guaranteed cure")
- Casual/informal tone for serious conditions
- Generic "years of experience" without specifics  - say "85+ international publications" instead

## Food & Beverage

**Voice:** Sensory + Emotional + Pride
**Register:** Informal (εσύ)
**Emotional/Rational mix:** 90% emotional (memory, tradition, taste), 10% rational

### Tone Markers
- Sensory language: γεύση, φρεσκάδα, βελούδινη αίσθηση, καθαρή απόλαυση
- Heritage/place: τόπος, παράδοση, οικογένεια, geographic specifics
- Emotional memory: μνήμη, "στην υγειά μας", "στην αυλή"

### Vocabulary Examples
- "Το τσίπουρο δεν είναι μόνο γεύση. Είναι μνήμη."
- "Ένα μικρό ποτήρι πάνω στο τραπέζι. Μετά το φαγητό, με τη μητέρα ή τον παππού."
- Reference specific ingredients, regions, methods

### Pain Point Approach
- Usually not problem-focused  - instead, aspiration/desire-focused
- "Ψάχνεις ποιότητα που δεν κάνει εκπτώσεις στη γεύση;"

### CTA Style
- "Αγόρασε τώρα" / "Ανακάλυψε τη γεύση" / "Παράγγειλε"
- Casual, warm

### What to Avoid
- Clinical language
- Generic "premium quality"  - describe the actual quality
- Translating emotional Greek phrases to stiff English

## SaaS / Tech

**Voice:** Confident + Data-driven + Clear
**Register:** Informal (both Greek and English)
**Emotional/Rational mix:** 30% emotional (frustration with current tools), 70% rational (data, ROI, efficiency)

### Tone Markers
- Results-quantified: "3X increase", "50% fewer", "20+ hours saved"
- Integration language: automate, workflow, seamless, real-time
- Clarity over cleverness

### Vocabulary Examples
- "Convert 10% of previously silent website visitors into leads"
- "Automation strategy + compelling copywriting = predictable conversions"
- Specific metrics always beat adjectives

### Pain Point Approach
- Problem/Solution flip pairs (strongest pattern)
- "What You're Losing by NOT Automating"
- Quantify the cost of inaction

### CTA Style
- "Book a Demo" / "Start Free Trial" / "See It in Action"
- "Ξεκίνα σήμερα" / "Ζήτησε Demo"

### What to Avoid
- Buzzword stacking: "cutting-edge", "state-of-the-art", "seamlessly", "revolutionize"
- Vague claims without numbers
- Overuse of "AI-powered" without explaining what it actually does

## B2B Services

**Voice:** Direct + Pragmatic + Problem-aware
**Register:** Formal (εσείς) in Greek, informal in English
**Emotional/Rational mix:** 40% emotional (brand reputation, competitive fear), 60% rational (efficiency, cost, reliability)

### Tone Markers
- Business impact language: ROI, efficiency, competitive advantage
- Problem-to-solution directness
- "Η εμπειρία παράδοσης που αξίζει το brand σου"

### Vocabulary Examples
- "400+ επιχειρήσεις στην Αττική μας εμπιστεύονται"
- "Same-day delivery σε όλη την Αθήνα"
- Specific deliverables and guarantees

### Pain Point Approach
- Competitive threat framing (strongest for B2B)
- "Αν ένα άλλο κατάστημα εγγυάται ταχύτερη παράδοση, θα το επιλέξουν."
- Cost-of-inaction quantification

### CTA Style
- "Ζήτησε πληροφορίες" / "Ξεκίνα σήμερα" / "Ας μιλήσουμε"
- "Book a Free Call" / "Get a Quote"

### What to Avoid
- Overly friendly/casual tone for serious B2B decisions
- Generic "solutions for your business"  - name the specific problem you solve
- Comparison tables that are obviously biased (all checkmarks vs all X marks)

## Lifestyle / Consumer

**Voice:** Aspirational + Personal + Empowering
**Register:** Informal (εσύ)
**Emotional/Rational mix:** 80% emotional (identity, confidence, self-care), 20% rational

### Tone Markers
- Self-care language: φροντίδα, ανανέωση, ισορροπία
- Empowerment: "αξίζεις", "η δική σου ρουτίνα"
- Identity reinforcement: "Τα μαλλιά σου, η ταυτότητά σου"

### Vocabulary Examples
- "Η μοναδική ολοκληρωμένη συμβουλευτική μαλλιών στην Ελλάδα"
- "Ρουτίνα που σε κάνει να αισθάνεσαι σίγουρη"
- Specific benefits over vague "luxury" or "premium"

### Pain Point Approach
- Recognition questions (strongest): "Πόσες φορές είπες..."
- Personal frustration scenarios
- "Σου έχει τύχει να σου καταστρέψουν τα μαλλιά;"

### CTA Style
- "Κλείσε συνεδρία" / "Ανακάλυψε τη σειρά" / "Ξεκίνα τη ρουτίνα σου"
- Warm, inviting, zero pressure

## Events

**Voice:** Energetic + FOMO + Casual
**Register:** Informal (εσύ), bilingual OK (Greeklish fine)
**Emotional/Rational mix:** 95% emotional (excitement, belonging, exclusivity), 5% rational

### Tone Markers
- Exclamation marks (used sparingly but allowed)
- Street/youth culture vocabulary
- FOMO creation: "που δεν πρέπει να χάσεις"
- Casual bilingual: English terms embedded in Greek text

### Vocabulary Examples
- "Events & DJ sets που δεν πρέπει να χάσεις"
- "Sneaker πολιτισμός στην Ελλάδα!"
- Name specific artists, brands, activities

### CTA Style
- "Αγόρασε το εισιτήριό σου!" / "Κράτησε θέση!"
- Urgency-driven, direct

## Parenting / Education

**Voice:** Empathetic + Guilt-reducing + Supportive
**Register:** Informal (εσύ)
**Emotional/Rational mix:** 75% emotional (guilt, hope, reassurance), 25% rational (expertise, method)

### Tone Markers
- Guilt reduction: "Δεν χρειάζεται να τα ξέρεις όλα"
- Validation: "Αυτό το άγχος είναι φυσιολογικό"
- Support language: "υποστήριξη", "δεν είσαι μόνος/η", "βοήθεια"

### Vocabulary Examples
- "Αρκεί να έχεις τη σωστή υποστήριξη"
- "Δεν χρειάζεται να τα ξέρεις όλα"
- Specific child development challenges, not generic "parenting"

### Pain Point Approach
- Emotional scenario with inner dialogue
- "'Δεν κάνω αρκετά;' 'Φταίω εγώ;'"
- Normalize the struggle before offering the solution

### CTA Style
- "Βρες την υποστήριξη που χρειάζεσαι" / "Κλείσε συνεδρία" / "Γίνε μέλος σήμερα"
- Warm, empathetic, never pushy

## Restaurant

**Voice:** Edgy + Appetizing + Local-pride
**Register:** Informal, bilingual (English phrases in Greek text)
**Emotional/Rational mix:** 85% emotional (vibe, taste, experience), 15% rational (menu, location, delivery)

### Tone Markers
- Confidence: "This is how Athens does pizza bars."
- Bilingual attitude: "If you know, you know."
- Sensory + social: "catch-up", "mood", "vibe"
- Playful/edgy: "Better than your ex, hotter than your next."

### CTA Style
- "Παράγγειλε τώρα" / "Order now" / "Κάνε κράτηση"
- Can be edgier than other industries

### What to Avoid
- Corporate language
- Generic "quality ingredients"  - describe the actual food
- Over-explaining the concept  - let the vibe speak
