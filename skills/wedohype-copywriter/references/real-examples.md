```
"Γιατί κάθε επίσκεψη στο κομμωτήριο μοιάζει με ρίσκο;"

- "Σου έχει τύχει να σου καταστρέψουν τα μαλλιά από λάθος τεχνική;"
```

### Comparison Table Anti-Patterns
- **Long descriptions in cells:** "Dashboard σε real-time. Βλέπεις κέρδη, ζημιές, τάσεις. Κάθε μέρα." → Too much text. Use "Dashboard σε real-time" or just ✓.
- **English business jargon in Greek copy:** "Financial Clarity", "Cash Flow Management", "Strategic Planning"
- **Assumed features:** Writing "Dashboard σε real-time" without knowing the brand actually offers one
- **Χύμα language:** "Ξέρεις αν βγαίνεις κάθε μήνα" sounds unprofessional. Better: "Πλήρης εικόνα κερδοφορίας"
- **Unreadable Greek:** "Κόστος κλάσμα full-time CFO" doesn't parse. Better: "Χωρίς κόστος full-time πρόσληψης"
- **Every row ✓/✗:** Some rows can be ✓/✓ or ✓/Maybe to feel honest
- **Too many rows (10+).** Keep it 5-8 for maximum impact.

## Anti-Examples (What NOT to Write)

### Weak Headlines
- "Transforming Leadership Through Expert Coaching" - Generic, no specificity
- "Empowering Transformation, Envisioning Success" - Double abstraction, zero value
- "Simplify your fishing trips with the SideSling" - Reads like a product manual
- "Our Journey to Excellence" - Self-congratulatory, reader doesn't care

### Weak CTAs
- "Explore Our Services" - Where am I going?
- "Read More" - Weakest possible CTA
- "View Programs" - Generic, no urgency
- "Work with us" - No specificity about what happens

### Weak Pain Points
- "Many businesses face challenges in today's competitive landscape" - Says nothing
- "You deserve better" - Empty without specifics
- "Common problems include..." - Boring setup

### Banned Phrases
- "Cutting-edge" / "State-of-the-art" / "Seamlessly" / "Revolutionize"
- "Comprehensive solution" / "Holistic approach"
- "Game-changer" (in testimonials)
- "Υψηλής ποιότητας" without proof
- "Λύσεις για κάθε ανάγκη"
- "Καλωσήρθατε στο website μας"
