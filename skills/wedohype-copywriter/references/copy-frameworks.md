# Copy Frameworks - Page Type Templates

Complete section-by-section templates for every page type. Each template shows the section flow, what to write in each section, and how sections connect.

---

## Landing Page - Service

Best for: consultants, agencies, medical practices, coaches, professional services.

**Section flow (10-12 sections):**

### Section 1 - Hero
- **Heading:** Benefit-promise or problem-reframe (8-15 words)
- **Subheading:** Expand on the promise with specifics (1-2 sentences)
- **CTA:** Primary action (the same CTA that repeats throughout)
- **Notes:** Hero image or illustration alongside. Provide 2-3 headline options.

*The headline should answer: "What will change in my life if I use this?"*

### Section 2 - Pain Points
- **Heading:** Question that triggers recognition: "Μήπως κάτι από αυτά σού είναι γνώριμο;" or "Say no to..."
- **Body:** 3-5 specific pain scenarios using audience language
- **Format:** Bullet list or numbered problems. Each must be recognizable, not abstract.

*Pain point framing approaches:*
- **Recognition questions:** "Πόσες φορές είπες 'Δεν βγήκε όπως το φανταζόμουν';"
- **"Say no to..." list:** "Πες όχι σε ανεπαρκή απολύμανση και κινδύνους υγιεινής."
- **Emotional scenario:** "Εκτοξεύσεις, άρνηση, δυσκολίες στον ύπνο... 'Δεν κάνω αρκετά;' 'Φταίω εγώ;'"
- **Competitive threat:** "Αν ένα άλλο κατάστημα εγγυάται ταχύτερη παράδοση, θα το επιλέξουν."
- **Problem/Solution flip:** Problem → one-line solution for each

### Section 3 - Solution
- **Heading:** Position your offering as the answer to Section 2
- **Body:** 1-2 sentences describing what the service does and why it works
- **CTA:** Repeat primary CTA

*The bridge between problems and benefits. Don't list features yet - just establish "here's the answer."*

### Section 4 - Benefits/Reasons
- **Heading:** "Γιατί [Brand]" or benefit-led statement
- **Body:** 4-6 benefits, each with:
 - **Subheading:** Benefit name (short, declarative)
 - **Body:** 1-2 sentences supporting the benefit with proof or specifics

*Lead with benefits, support with features. "Απόλυτη υγιεινή σε κάθε κύκλο πλύσης" (benefit) → "Σταθερή απολύμανση σε θερμοκρασίες έως 93°C" (feature proof).*

### Section 5 - CTA + Form
- **Heading:** Action-oriented or emotional appeal
- **Body:** 1 sentence removing friction
- **CTA:** Primary action
- **Form fields:** Name, Email, Phone, Message (keep minimal - max 4-5 fields)

### Section 6 - Target Audience (optional)
- **Heading:** "Για ποιον είναι η [service];" or "Who is this for?"
- **Body:** 3-5 audience personas described in 1 sentence each

*Include this when the service has multiple audience segments.*

### Section 7 - How It Works / Process
- **Heading:** "Πώς λειτουργεί" or "3 απλά βήματα"
- **Body:** 3-4 steps, each with:
 - **Step name:** Short verb phrase
 - **Description:** 1 sentence explaining what happens

*Steps should feel effortless. The message is: "It's simpler than you think."*

### Section 8 - Testimonials
- **Heading:** "Τι λένε οι πελάτες μας" or social proof metric
- **Body:** 2-3 testimonials formatted as:
 - Quote (2-3 sentences, specific result or emotion)
 - Name + title/role
- **Notes:** Include multi-platform ratings if available (Google, Doctoranytime, etc.)

### Section 9 - Comparison Table (optional)
- **Heading:** "[Brand] ή μόνος/η σου;" or "[Brand] vs Οι υπόλοιποι"
- **Format:** Native table, 3 columns: Θέμα/Feature | [Brand] | Οι υπόλοιποι/Μόνος σου
- **Body:** 5-7 rows. Each row is a **short phrase** (one line max). Columns 2-3 use **✓ / ✗** marks.
- **Rules:** No long descriptions in cells. No assumed features. No English jargon in Greek copy. Professional but human tone. See section-copy-patterns.md for detailed guidance.

*Include when the service has direct competitors or a DIY alternative. Especially useful for consultants, agencies, and professional services.*

### Section 10 - FAQ
- **Heading:** "Συχνές Ερωτήσεις"
- **Body:** 6-10 questions. Mix:
 - 40% practical/logistical ("Πόσο διαρκεί;" / "Πόσο κοστίζει;")
 - 35% objection-handling ("Γιατί να έρθω σε εσάς;")
 - 25% educational ("Τι είναι η [service];")
- **Close:** "Δεν βρήκες την απάντηση; Επικοινώνησε μαζί μας!"

### Section 11 - Final CTA
- **Heading:** Most emotionally charged headline of the page
- **Body:** 1-2 sentences - urgency or reassurance
- **CTA:** Primary action (same as Hero)

*This is the last chance. Make the headline hit harder than the Hero.*

## Landing Page - Product

Best for: physical products, D2C brands, equipment, consumer goods.

**Section flow (9-11 sections):**

### Section 1 - Hero
- **Heading:** Product positioning statement or category-defining claim
- **Subheading:** What makes this product different (unique mechanism)
- **CTA:** Primary purchase/discovery action

### Section 2 - Problem / Why This Product
- **Heading:** The problem the product solves or the standard it challenges
- **Body:** 3-4 pain points about existing alternatives

### Section 3 - Product Features
- **Heading:** Benefit-led, not "Features"
- **Body:** 4-6 features, each with:
 - **Subheading:** Benefit name
 - **Body:** 1-2 sentences with specific proof (numbers, certifications, ingredients)
- **Notes:** Alternating layout - image + text, switch sides

### Section 4 - Science / Quality / Ingredients
- **Heading:** The "why it works" proof section
- **Body:** Technical credibility - certifications, lab results, sourcing, process

*"Υψηλής ποιότητας" is banned. Instead: "ISO 9001 certified" / "93°C sanitization" / "0% parabens, vegan, cruelty-free"*

### Section 5 - Comparison Table
- **Heading:** "[Brand] vs παραδοσιακές εναλλακτικές" or "Δες τη διαφορά" or "[Brand] ή μόνος/η σου;"
- **Format:** Native table, 3 columns: Θέμα/Feature | [Brand] | Οι υπόλοιποι
- **Body:** 5-7 rows. Each row is a **short phrase** (one line max). Columns 2-3 use **✓ / ✗** marks.
- **Rules:** No long descriptions in cells. No assumed features. No English jargon in Greek copy. Professional but human tone. See section-copy-patterns.md for detailed guidance.

### Section 6 - Testimonials / UGC
- **Heading:** Real customer results
- **Body:** 2-4 testimonials. For products, include:
 - Before/after results when possible
 - Specific product used
 - Named customer or "Verified buyer"

### Section 7 - As Seen In / Press
- **Heading:** "Έγραψαν για εμάς" or "As Seen In"
- **Notes:** Logo bar of publications/press

### Section 8 - FAQ
- 8-12 questions, heavier on practical (shipping, returns, ingredients, usage)

### Section 9 - Newsletter / Lead Capture
- **Heading:** Offer-driven: "10% έκπτωση στην πρώτη σου παραγγελία"
- **Body:** 1 sentence value proposition
- **CTA:** "Εγγραφή" or "Θέλω 10% έκπτωση!"
- **Form:** Email only

## Homepage - Service Business

Best for: agencies, professional firms, multi-service businesses.

**Section flow (8-10 sections):**

### Section 1 - Hero
- **Heading:** What the business does + who it serves, with attitude
- **Subheading:** Expand or qualify
- **CTA:** Primary action

*Homepage heroes are more about positioning than a single offer.*

### Section 2 - Services Overview
- **Heading:** "Τι κάνουμε" or benefit-led
- **Body:** 3-5 service cards, each:
 - **Service name**
 - **1-sentence description**
 - **Optional CTA** per service

### Section 3 - Why Us / Differentiators
- **Heading:** Unique positioning claim
- **Body:** 3-6 reasons with proof points

### Section 4 - About Teaser
- **Heading:** Brand personality statement
- **Body:** 2-3 sentences - the origin story in brief
- **CTA:** "Γνώρισέ μας" or "Η ιστορία μας"

### Section 5 - Process (optional)
- 3-4 steps showing how working with you works

### Section 6 - Social Proof
- Stats bar: key numbers (clients, projects, results)
- Client/partner logos
- 2-3 testimonials

### Section 7 - Blog Teaser (optional)
- **Heading:** "Από το blog μας"
- **Notes:** 3 recent post cards

### Section 8 - FAQ
- 6-8 questions focused on working with the company

### Section 9 - Final CTA + Contact
- **Heading:** Warm, inviting
- **CTA:** Primary + contact info

## Ecommerce Homepage

Best for: online stores, D2C brands, product catalogs.

**Section flow (10-12 sections):**

### Section 1 - Hero + CTA
- **Heading:** Brand mission or hero product
- **CTA:** Shop/Explore primary category

### Section 2 - Categories
- 3-6 product category cards with images

### Section 3 - Best Sellers
- 3-6 product highlights

### Section 4 - USPs / Science / Quality
- 3-4 brand differentiators (ingredients, process, certifications)

### Section 5 - Testimonials / UGC
- Customer reviews, Instagram embeds, before/after

### Section 6 - As Seen In
- Press logos

### Section 7 - Founders (optional)
- Brief founder story connecting to brand mission

### Section 8 - Blog
- 3 recent post cards

### Section 9 - FAQ
- 8-12 questions (shipping, returns, ingredients, sustainability)

### Section 10 - Newsletter
- Discount-driven email capture

## About Page

Best for: any business that needs a story. Most important page for trust-building.

**Section flow (5-7 sections):**

### Section 1 - Cover Statement
- **Heading:** Bold identity claim (not "About Us")
- **Body:** 1-2 sentences - what the company stands for

*Example: "Ξεκίνησε με έναν καφέ στο Πανεπιστήμιο Πατρών. Σήμερα αλλάζει τον τρόπο που φροντίζουμε το δέρμα μας."*

### Section 2 - Origin Story
- **Body:** 3-5 paragraphs telling how and why the company was founded
- **Tone:** Personal, narrative, human. Use names, places, specific moments.

*This is the longest section in any document. Let it breathe. The story should make the reader feel they know the founder.*

### Section 3 - Philosophy / Mission
- **Heading:** What drives the company
- **Body:** 2-3 sentences - beliefs, not corporate jargon

### Section 4 - Team / Founder
- Bio per key team member: Name, role, 1-2 sentences, photo
- Or founder spotlight with credentials

### Section 5 - Goals / Vision
- Forward-looking statement

### Section 6 - Press / Awards (optional)
- Logo bar + notable mentions

### Section 7 - CTA
- **Heading:** Warm, personal invitation
- **CTA:** "Ας γνωριστούμε!" / "Work with us"
